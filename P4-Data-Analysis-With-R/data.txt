Score Rating of 18000 game from IGN

Description
a Dataset containing the score and other attributes of more than 18000 games. The variables are as follows:

- X: this is just an index
- url: url of game
- title: title of game
- score_phrase 
- score
- platform
- genre
- editors_choice: Y(selected as editor's choice) or N (not selected)
- release_year
- release_month
- release_day

Reference:
https://www.kaggle.com/egrinstein/20-years-of-games
